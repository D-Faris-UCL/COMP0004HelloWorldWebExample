# Understanding Tetanus

Tetanus is a potentially life-threatening infection caused by bacteria that can enter the body through cuts or wounds.

It is vital to stay on top of your tetanus vaccinations to prevent the onset of symptoms.

## Tetanus Vaccination

A tetanus vaccine booster is recommended every 10 years.

## First Aid for Wounds

In case of injury, immediately clean the wound thoroughly with soap and water to reduce the risk of infection.
